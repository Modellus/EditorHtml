Our FAQ covers the most common topics about using Modellus — from subscription plans and marketplace credits to sharing models and working with students.

Explore Modellus

01. What is Modellus and how does it work?

Modellus is an interactive platform for building and exploring mathematical and scientific models. You write equations just like on paper, and Modellus instantly translates them into animated simulations, charts, and tables — making abstract concepts tangible and easy to explore in real time.

02. Do I need to install anything to use Modellus?

No installation is required. Modellus runs entirely in your web browser — just open the editor and start building. You can even try it without creating an account. If you sign in, your models are saved to the cloud so you can access them from any device.

03. How does the monthly marketplace credit work?

Marketplace credit is added to your account at the start of each billing cycle. Standard plan subscribers receive €5 and Premium plan subscribers receive €15. Credits can be used to purchase premium content created by the Modellus team and community educators. Unused credits do not roll over.

04. Can I share my models with others?

Yes! All plans include the ability to share your models. You can make a model public so anyone can view and interact with it, or share a direct link with specific people. Teachers often share models directly with their class.

05. I'm a teacher. Can I use Modellus with my students?

Absolutely. Modellus is designed with educators in mind. You can create models that your students interact with, share worksheets, and build a library of content tailored to your curriculum. Many teachers use Modellus for both in-class and remote learning.

06. What is the AI assistant, and how can I use it?

The AI assistant is available in Standard and Premium plans. It helps you build models faster by interpreting natural language descriptions and generating the equations and model structure for you. Simply describe the concept you want to simulate and the AI will set it up.
