Media Shape

The Media shape embeds images on the board as backgrounds, illustrations, or visual context for your model.

Screenshot: Media shape on the board

Overview

A Media shape places an image on the board canvas. Images can serve as backgrounds (e.g., a photograph of an experiment), illustrations, or visual context. The shape supports standard image formats and can be positioned, scaled, and layered with other shapes.
